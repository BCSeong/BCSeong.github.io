Professional Summary

Optical and Computational Imaging Engineer with a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering and over six years of experience developing high-precision optical inspection and metrology systems. Specialized in computational optics, interferometric imaging, and inline inspection solutions. Currently leading industrial R&D at Koh Young Technology with international collaboration (AIT, Fraunhofer IPM). Strong ability to translate advanced optical concepts into robust, cost-effective solutions for high-throughput semiconductor and industrial inspection.
